ART WITH HEART

This website was designed for a company that provides children and parents with resources for creative expression. Our job was to Migrate their original website from a Python/Django based stack to a Wordpress using Elementor. I was tasked with implementing CSS and JS that was beyond the scope of Elementor.

PHOTO SNAG

This app was designed to allow users to find the best spots to shoot photography in a given location using a google and flickr API. The app was modeled after instagrams 3 column grid and is easy to use.

SFIM

This website was designed for a company called SFIM. It was created to fix their messy site and make it quick and simple for parents to get information about the school and sign their kids up for classes. I designed and developed the website.

HAFA ADAI LEMONADE

This website has created for the company Hafa Adai Lemonade, in order to boost awareness of their food truck and sell merchandise online. In order to do so, our team created a single page e-commerce website, that included an order request form and an easy access social media widget. The design revolved around a modern and clean style that got straight to the point.

TEAM UNI

This website was designed around the intent of creating a more design savvy informational website for web designers and coders. The design was chosen to illustrate the modern and dynamic use of HTML5 and CSS3. I worked on the base layout and home page of the website.

ZEN GARDEN

This page was solely created to showcase the possible utilization of the new functions of CSS3. The design chosen was to give the user the feeling of being in space while things are constantly floating around in motion.

JOE LINARES PHOTOGRAPHY

This website was created as a concept depicting entering a coffee shop and and being able to view the art. The website showcases photography photos if they were hung on the walls of a coffee shop. Desaturated and pastel tones were used to give it the modern cozy vibe.
